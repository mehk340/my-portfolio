About Me

Most developers see a feature request. I see the business problem hiding behind it. That's because before I wrote a single line of production code, I spent years in operations and HR management — understanding how teams work, where workflows break, and what leaders actually need to make decisions.

I bridge that gap. When you work with me, you get a developer who asks "what outcome does this create?" before opening a text editor. The result is software that fits your business like a glove — not a generic tool awkwardly repurposed.
